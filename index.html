SBG O&M SAFETY DEPARTMENT

Our Vision

SBG O&M Safety Department delivered its performance to make a safe work place on the world’s largest projects like Harramain Makkah and Madina, Development King Abdul Aziz Endowments, Palaces, Hospitals and Airports etc. We are Constantly Delivering the quality of work for which our clients are happy & satisfied. Dedication, hard work, high level training, continued research and development are the key factors behind our success. Our dedicated and well trained Safety Staffs, Safety Equipped with up-to-date technology, are always in a position to serve and achieve client’s satisfaction on Project. SBG O&M Safety Department is to keep meeting client’s expectations and enhance their satisfaction by continually improving performance and providing comprehensive diversified safety services leading to reduction in customer’s expenditure.



Mr. Samir Jamjoom (EBM) leading the SBG O&M Safety management is committed towards health and safety at work place and we also consider it our moral, legal and financial obligation to protect project staff, visitor and sub-contractors from Injury, Loss and Damages to the project property. 

HSE policy

To improve the Standard Operating Procedures in all the project sites by following the safety guidelines from the Civil Defense, NFPA & Also according to the international safety regulatory organizations (OSHA, ISO& OHSAS). SBG O&M believes in developing safe working procedures and maintaining a 0 injury working environment.
